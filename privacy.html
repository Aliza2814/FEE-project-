The information we collect is used in the following ways:

Ingredient Alchemy may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

6. Your Choices

You can control how we use your information in the following ways:
